Lab 28 On the Web

Goals

  • Visit and share your website!

Visit Your Website!

Wait a few minutes and enter the name of your GitHub repository as a URL. <username>.github.io will show your HTML website in all its glory, fully hosted and shareable to anyone with that URL!

GitHub lets you host as many static websites as you would like from your GitHub account. As long as you have an index.html in the repository, it will show up on the web. The URLs for any other repository on your account will be <username>.github.io/<project-name>.

Share Your Website

Go to GitHub Discussions and in the Share Your Website! discussion post, make a reply with a link to your website. Feel free to tell us a little about it or talk about your progress so far!
