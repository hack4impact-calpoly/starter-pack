Main, Article, and Section

Inside of your HTML document, there are a few ways to provide clear meaning to the structure of your content. HTML provides us with the <main>, <article>, and <section> tags.

The <main> tag is used once to signify the most important content on the page. The <article> tag can be used once like in a blog or multiple times like in a newspaper to signify a blocks of content. And of course, <section> is a more general divisor which can break up <article> or represent chapters.
