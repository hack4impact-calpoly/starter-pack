The 3rd Annual 24 Hour Game Creation Challenge
2009-11-01T12:00:00.000Z
The 3rd Annual 24 Hour Game Creation Challenge is coming up on November 7th, 2009. The 24 Hour Game Creation Challenge is a competition where teams of up to 4 people have 24 hours to create a game from scratch. The competition is open to all game development tools and languages. The competition is free to enter and there are prizes for the top 3 teams. The competition is sponsored by GarageGames, Microsoft, and Adobe. For more information, visit the competition website at www.24hrgame.com.
