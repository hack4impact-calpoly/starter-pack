Margins and Padding

Both margins and padding have a shorthand that amalgamates multiple properties into one property. This shorthand property takes a space-separated string of values instead of just one.

The four margin properties, margin-top, margin-right, margin-bottom, and margin-left can all be condensed into the margin property. The order of the margin property values is margin: top right bottom left. The following definition shows this:


      
Note: If you only put two values instead of four, margin: 5px 10px, the first will be used for both top and bottom
        and the second for left and right.

      
The same format of shorthand works for combining padding-top, padding-right, padding-bottom, and padding-left into padding.
      

      
Borders

      
border-width, border-style, and border-color can all be combined into one border
        property. This shorthand combination can also be applied to specific border edges i.e. border-top, border-right, border-bottom, and border-left.
